Techopedia is an annual technical fest organized by the student chapter of IEEE SIES GST.

Each and every participant will be awarded with a certificate on participation in every event. Winners will get National level certificates.

MS Teams will be used for all the events.

You can click on more info to know in detail about events.

You can register for any of our events by contacting to our event heads or respective volunteers or by just clicking on the form's Link given in Schedule section of the page.

No on-day registrations will be entertained.
